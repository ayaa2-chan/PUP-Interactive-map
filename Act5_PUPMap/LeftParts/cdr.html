Charlie Del Rosario Building
14.597193, 121.009917

The Charlie Del Rosario Building, named after the late PUP professor and activist Charlie Del Rosario, is one of the main academic structures in the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Mesa. It houses several college departments, laboratories, and classrooms that support different academic programs.

Significance and Features:

  • Built to honor the legacy of Charlie Del Rosario, remembered for his advocacy for education and student welfare.
