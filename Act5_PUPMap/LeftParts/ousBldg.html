PUP Open University System
14.597558, 121.009502

The PUP Open University System (OUS) Building is one of the newly constructed facilities in the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Mesa. It serves as the home of the university’s distance and flexible learning programs, designed to provide accessible education to learners across the country. The modern structure reflects PUP’s continued efforts to expand digital and lifelong learning opportunities.
