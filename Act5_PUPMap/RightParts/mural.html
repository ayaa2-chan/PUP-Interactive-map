PUP Mural
14.599063, 121.011805

The PUP Mural is a cut and welded brass mural relief measuring 2.5 x 9.3 meters, created by National Artist Eduardo Castrillo in 1974. The artwork, titled “Consolidated Growth through Education,” represents the vital role of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines in shaping the educational development of the youth as they prepare to contribute to nation-building.

Significance and Features:

  • Depicts the social, economic, industrial, technological, and cultural aspects of life that intertwine to form the foundation of national progress.
  • Serves as a tribute to education and the transformative power of learning, embodying PUP’s enduring mission to nurture socially responsible citizens.
  • Symbolizes the purposeful growth and development of the Filipino youth, whom the University recognizes as the builders of the nation’s future.
