PUP P.E. and Nutrition and Food Technology Research Building
PUP P.E. Building, Teresa, Santa Mesa, Manila, 1008 Metro Manila

The PUP Physical Education (PE) Building is the center for sports, physical fitness, and wellness activities. It embodies the university’s vision of holistic education — developing both the mind and body of every student.

Significance and Features:

  • Main venue for physical education programs, sports preparation, and wellness initiatives
  • Contains an indoor fitness area with barbells, weights, pull-up bars, and other exercise equipment for training
