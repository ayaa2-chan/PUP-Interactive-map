PUP CatWalk
14.598946, 121.011608

The PUP Catwalk is a long walkway that stretches directly from the Main Gate of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Mesa to the PUP Obelisk. It serves as the main pedestrian pathway for students, faculty, and visitors entering the campus. The Catwalk offers a refreshing canopy of trees that provide shade throughout the day, making it one of the most relaxing and scenic spots in the university.

Significance and Features:

  • The surrounding greenery and benches make it an ideal spot for rest, casual talks, and photography.
  • Provides study areas, computer sections, and discussion rooms, promoting both individual and collaborative learning.
  • Serves as the first view for anyone entering PUP Sta. Mesa, highlighting the Obelisk straight ahead.
